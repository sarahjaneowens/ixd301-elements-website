HE-LI-UM

Helium is a chemical element with the symbol (He) and has the atomic number of 2. It is a colourless, odourless, tasteless, non-toxic gas.

Helium is the first in the noble gas family in the periodic table. This family is known as the colour purple. It's boiling point is the lowest among all the elements.
